## Diagnostic Inventory: Navigation Map

### 1. Main Menu Structure
*   **Inventory**
    *   `Dashboard`: High-level alerts.
    *   `Setup`:
        *   `Item Master`: Reagents, Consumables, etc.
        *   `Store Setup`: Branches and locations.
        *   `Mapping`: Reagent to Machine/Test.
    *   `Transactions`:
        *   `GRN`: Receiving stock.
        *   `Issue`: Internal distribution.
        *   `Open Log`: Reagent unsealing.
        *   `Adjustment`: Corrections.
    *   `Reports`:
        *   `Stock Ledger`: History.
        *   `Expiry Report`: Critical alerts.
        *   `Consumption`: Usage analysis.
