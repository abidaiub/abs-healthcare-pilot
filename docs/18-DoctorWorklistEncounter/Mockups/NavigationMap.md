## Doctor Worklist & Encounter: Navigation Map

### 1. Main Menu Structure
*   **Clinical (Doctor View)**
    *   `Worklist`: Daily patient queue.
    *   `Encounter Dashboard`: Active consultation.
    *   `Patient History`: Searchable archive of past encounters.
    *   `Clinical Setup`:
        *   `Note Templates`: Pre-defined SOAP templates.
        *   `Diagnosis Favorites`: Frequently used ICD-10 codes.
    *   `Reports`:
        *   `Productivity`: My daily/monthly stats.
        *   `Disease Trends`: Common diagnoses in my practice.
        *   `Follow-up List`: Patients due for return visits.
