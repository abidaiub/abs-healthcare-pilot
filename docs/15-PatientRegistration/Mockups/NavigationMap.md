## Patient Registration: Navigation Map

### 1. Main Menu Structure
*   **Patient Management**
    *   `Search / Directory`: Main entry point.
    *   `New Registration`: Direct access to the form.
    *   `MPI Tools`:
        *   `Merge Patients`: Admin tool for duplicates.
        *   `ID Card Printing`: Bulk or single card generation.
    *   `Reports`:
        *   `Daily Registration`: New patients today.
        *   `Demographic Analysis`: Age/Gender/Area distribution.
        *   `Status Report`: Active vs. Deceased.
