## Patient Profile & Ledger: Navigation Map

### 1. Main Menu Structure
*   **Patient 360**
    *   `Search Patient`: Find profile by MRN/Phone/Name.
    *   `Profile Overview`: Demographics, Alerts, and Summary.
    *   `Clinical Ledger`: Chronological medical events.
    *   `Financial Ledger`: Detailed account statement.
    *   `Journey Timeline`: Visual path of patient care.
    *   `Family Links`: Manage related patient records.
    *   `Reports`:
        *   `Patient Statement`: Financial printout.
        *   `Clinical Summary`: Medical history printout.
        *   `Due Summary`: List of all outstanding dues for this patient.
